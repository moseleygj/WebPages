Meet Our Pastors
Apostles Sherwayne and Theresa Williams, hail from the beautiful twin islands of Trinidad and Tobago. From a young age, they exhibited a deep sense of compassion and a strong connection to their faith. Growing up in a close-knit community, they were inspired by their faith to join ministries to serve the call that was upon their lives.

 Their commitment to the gospel is evident in their involvement with church initiatives addressing poverty, education, and equality. With fifty plus years combined in ministry they have forged partnerships with community and church leaders, fostering a collaborative spirit that transcends denominational boundaries. Over the years their ministry would expand overseas, establishing the Azar International United States branch in New York City.

  Their sermons are characterized by a blend of profound spirituality and practical wisdom. Under their transformative leadership, the church has blossomed into a vibrant community hub, offering support to the less fortunate and becoming a catalyst for positive change. Throughout the challenges of ministry, they remain by God's grace to be a force in the Christian community.

 Their mission to surround, support, help and protect is carried out each and every day as they continue to transform lives by the power of the Holy Ghost. As they create more disciples in the Earth, they have truly become fishers of men. Join them in fellowship on Saturdays at 9:30am, 1403 Eastern Parkway.
