AZAR Ministries International

We are a Christain faith-based Church. Our mission is to reach people and build relationships. At AZAR Ministries International Church, we place a high emphasis on helping you and your family connect with God. We want everyone, everywhere to have a relationship with the Holy Father.
Youth Fellowship
Youth fellowship is a weekely group meeting with younger members of the church, where they can discuss issues and get guidence, to work on self-emporovement.

1 Timothy 4:12 says, “Don't let anyone look down on you because you are young, but set an example for the believers in speech, in conduct, in love, in faith and in purity.”
Men's Fellowship
Helps facilitate the gathering of Christian men for the purpose of sharing experiences, encouragement, support and biblical study. The resultant of such a fellowship is men who are strong, encouraged, fortified, better husbands, fathers and men living a life that exemplifies Jesus Christ.
Women's Fellowship
The aim of the “Women's Fellowship” is to help the women in the church to lead exemplary lives as Christian women in love and unity, so that at home, in the market, and the public, their behaviour will attract other women to Christ.
Other Services
At Azar Ministries we offer many services such as baptism, personal/family counsel, group trips, parties, communion, clothing donation, choir, charity and food services
